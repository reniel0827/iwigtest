About

A house built on
hair integrity.

iWIG sources premium human hair from a curated network of ethical suppliers across Asia, Europe, and South America. Every bundle is hand-inspected, hand-tied, and quality-graded before it enters our wholesale catalog.

We exist to support the salons, boutiques, and stylists who refuse to compromise on what they sell. That's why we keep our trade gated: applicants must hold a valid business license, and every approved retailer gets the same wholesale tier — no inflated minimums, no preferential games.

What we believe

  • Quality first. Every SKU is QA'd twice before fulfillment.
  • Transparent sourcing. Origin documentation provided on request.
  • Trade respect. We don't sell to end consumers. Period.
